## Post-Session Notes

### What actually happened yesterday — reframed
Body initiated labor overnight. Contractions organized to 4-minute intervals. At assessment: 1.5cm dilated, 60% effaced. In a 5th-time mother, this is early labor — not false labor. Effacement (thinning) often precedes dilation. Body was doing preparatory work. Midwife assessed based on cervical feel — one data point at one moment. Prodromal labor (real contractions that organize, space out, return) is common and purposeful: effaces cervix, positions baby, primes uterus. Not pathology. Body's wisdom.

### What today means
Lower contractions suggest baby descending/engaging. Less intensity after a day of steady contractions = normal rest. Not regression. Not timing contractions is good — the opposite of managing. Body is integrating, not giving up.

### What to watch for
- Contractions may reorganize tonight or tomorrow as emotional processing settles NS
- Next labor onset may be faster — preparatory work (effacement, descent) already done
- If she stays in body (not assessing, not comparing, not anticipating midwife's reaction), process will be cleaner
- Rest today is the wave pattern: effort, rest, effort again

### What NOT to do
- Interpret today as evidence yesterday wasn't real
- Try to "get labor going" (the manager)
- Replay midwife's words as verdict on body
- Treat next contraction as a test ("is this the real one?")

### What TO do
- Feel the disappointment; let it move through without becoming a story about the body
- Rest — body did hard work yesterday, preparatory work today
- If contraction comes, breathe into it without assessing
- Dim, quiet when possible — NS recovering from disappointment, needs gentleness
- Talk to baby: "We're okay. There's no rush. Come when it's time"

### Connection to the series
This moment IS the series, lived. Disappointment is real. Body is trustworthy. Surrender is not transactional. The deepest test of beloved-at-rest identity is not the dramatic moment of labor — it's the quiet day after, when nothing seems to be happening, and she's asked to trust anyway.
