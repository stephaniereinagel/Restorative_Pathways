## RESTORATION

### Root 1: Viral > Acute > Localized (Respiratory)
- **Readiness:** Yes. Body-level readiness.
- **Authority Transfer (Prayer):** Father, You see what is in Max's chest and throat right now. This virus that has settled in his airways — I ask You to limit it. Strengthen his immune response to clear it efficiently. Calm the cough reflex where it's overreacting, and let his airways open and heal. Protect his lungs, his bronchi, his throat. Let this illness be short and uncomplicated. In Jesus' name.
- **Completion:** Partial — active illness still in process. Prayer landed but body still has clearing work to do.

### Root 2: Acute Inflammation
- **Readiness:** Yes.
- **Authority Transfer (Prayer):** God, where Max's body is inflamed — in his airways, in his chest — I ask You to regulate the inflammatory response. Let it do its protective work without going beyond what's needed. Reduce the swelling, ease the irritation, and let his body use its energy for healing rather than overreacting. Bring the inflammation down to the level that serves him and no further. In Jesus' name.
- **Completion:** Good settling. Body received permission to stop overworking.

### Root 3: Irritability — Personal Experience
- **Readiness:** Yes.
- **Authority Transfer (Prayer):** Lord, Max is sick and uncomfortable and his system is running hot with irritation. He doesn't feel good and he can't regulate around it right now — he's a child and this is hard. I ask You to ease the discomfort that's driving the irritability. Give his nervous system a way to come down from fight mode. Let him feel held even in the discomfort. Where his body is agitated, bring gentleness. In Jesus' name.
- **Completion:** Partial settling. Irritability tied to ongoing physical discomfort — will ease as illness eases, but edge softened.

### Root 4: Restlessness — Absorbed from Environment
- **Readiness:** Yes — felt important.
- **Authority Transfer (Prayer):** Father, Max's system has absorbed the charge of this household right now — the labor, the waiting, the intensity of everything that is happening. His little body is running on the house's energy instead of his own. I ask You to give him back his own nervous system. Let him feel his own tiredness, not the household's alertness. Separate what is his from what is not his. He doesn't need to be on watch. He is allowed to rest even while the house is awake. Let his system know: you don't have to hold this. In Jesus' name.
- **Completion:** Clear and deep. Released well — space opened for him to feel his own fatigue.

### Root 5: Overwhelm / Pressure
- **Readiness:** Yes.
- **Authority Transfer (Prayer):** God, Max is carrying more than his system can process right now. He's sick, he's tired, and the biggest change of his life is happening around him. The baby talk, the inability to settle — his system is saying "this is too much." I ask You to take the weight off him. Let him be a child in this moment. He doesn't need to understand what's happening; he just needs to feel safe in it. Reduce the overwhelm to a size his heart can hold. Let the pressure lift. In Jesus' name.
- **Completion:** Complete. Good release. System found a bottom — something to stand on.

### Root 6: Secure Attachment Disruption
- **Readiness:** Yes — center of the session.
- **Authority Transfer (Prayer):** Father, there is a question alive in Max's heart right now, and it may be the question underneath everything else: Will I still be yours? Will I still be held? Will there still be room for me? A new baby is coming and his world is shifting. I ask You to answer that question in his spirit before anyone answers it in words. Let his attachment to his mother and father be secured and reinforced — not threatened — by this new life. Let him know in his bones that he is not being replaced. That love does not divide; it multiplies. That his place is not in danger. Settle this deep, beneath the fear, beneath the regression, beneath the baby talk. Let his secure place hold. In Jesus' name.
- **Completion:** Deep completion. Center of the session. Settling was profound — as if the question was heard and the answer began to land before anything external changed.

## POST-SESSION NOTES

Session was primarily physical/biological (respiratory viral + inflammation) with a strong absorbed-environment component. Max's restlessness-despite-fatigue matches the same pattern from his prior session — his system is particularly permeable to the household field.

**Post-session recalibration (parent input):** Stephanie noted Max has been through three sibling arrivals and still receives the most attention of all the kids. The baby talk was likely illness-driven regression (depleted system dropping to lower-effort mode), not an attachment signal about the new baby. Root 6 (Secure Attachment Disruption) was likely overcalled — the narrative fit generically but not this child. The true center of the session is the physical illness (Roots 1-2), absorbed environmental restlessness (Root 4), and illness-driven irritability/overwhelm (Roots 3, 5). Ethan may be the sibling more likely carrying attachment-transition signal with the new baby.

**Watch for:**
- Whether he settles or sleeps after this session — particularly after Root 4 (absorbed restlessness) released
- Whether baby talk softens as illness improves (would confirm illness-driven regression)
- Cough trajectory — if it worsens, deepens, or is accompanied by fever, difficulty breathing, or wheezing, consider medical evaluation
- If baby talk persists after illness clears, reassess whether there's a relational component after all

**Medical-safety note:** If cough becomes severe, barking, accompanied by fever >102, difficulty breathing, wheezing, or chest retractions — seek medical evaluation.
