## Practical Treatment Recommendations

### Immediate (today)
- **Stop.** Not "slow down." Stop. Cancel or delegate what is possible.
- **Hydrate aggressively** — warm water with lemon + pinch of sea salt, bone broth, herbal tea
- **Vitamin C** — 1000mg now, then 500mg every 2-3 hours (to bowel tolerance); liposomal if available
- **Zinc** — 30mg with food; zinc lozenges ideal for upper respiratory (direct throat contact)
- **Vitamin D** — 4000-5000 IU if not already at pregnancy supplementation levels
- **Raw honey + warm water/tea** — soothes throat tickle, mild antimicrobial
- **Rest** — actual horizontal rest, not sitting while managing things on phone

### Next 24-48 hours
- Sleep as much as possible — immune function peaks during sleep
- Warm liquids continuously — broth, tea, warm water; avoid cold drinks
- Light food only — broth, simple soups, fruit; let digestion rest
- Steam inhalation if sinuses congest — hot water in bowl, towel over head
- Monitor: fever = body doing deeper work; support rather than suppress unless exceeding 101.5F in late pregnancy (contact care provider)

### What not to do
- Do not push through
- Do not take on "just a few things"
- Do not feel guilty about resting (that guilt is Root 1 talking)
