## Reading

Body started labor at 12:30am. Clean, progressing pattern overnight (8 min → 2.5–3 min). Pattern disrupted when morning environment changed: kids woke, emotional demands began, cognitive manager activated. Cortisol entered the system and competed with oxytocin — contractions became sporadic.

Not confusion. Not false labor. A body responding honestly to a split in attention. Oxytocin flows in darkness, stillness, safety. Cortisol from managing/responding disrupts the hormonal cascade.

Kids' emotional state not coincidental — children sense transitions. Pulling on the one person they always pull on. Old relational contract ("I must be needed") still the pattern the children know.
